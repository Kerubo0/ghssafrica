⁠EARGHSS 2026 CONCEPT NOTE

EARGHSS 2026 is curated to advance the success of the inaugural EARGHSS 2025 held on January 28th–30th, 2025, in Mombasa, Kenya and its resolution ('communiqué') to hold a successive summit in 2026 and establish EARGHSS as an annual Eastern Africa Regional summit.
